About Me

I’m Vivian Ikechukwu, a biochemistry graduate, certified virtual assistant, and aspiring data scientist with a passion for health and technology. My career path has given me hands-on experience as a customer care representative and assistant finance manager, where I honed key skills in communication, cost control, inventory tracking, and bank reconciliation. I’m detail-oriented, curious, and committed to continuous learning, always ready to grow and contribute wherever I am..
